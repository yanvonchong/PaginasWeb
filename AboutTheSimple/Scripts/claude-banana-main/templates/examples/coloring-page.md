# Coloring Page

## Domain Mode
Print Design

## Technique
Color Reference Dual-Output

## Prompt Template

A coloring page layout divided into two sections. The main section, occupying roughly eighty percent of the image, presents {scene} as a clean black-and-white line drawing on white paper, designed for {target_audience} to color in. The line work has {complexity} complexity, with clear outlines of varying thickness — thicker outer contours and thinner interior detail lines — and no filled-in black areas or shading, leaving all enclosed shapes completely white and ready for coloring. The drawing is themed around {theme}, with enough variety in shapes and textures to make coloring engaging — different patterns on clothing, varied foliage types, architectural details, and textural elements like fur, scales, brick, or fabric weave hinted through line work alone. In the lower right corner, a small rectangular inset — roughly fifteen percent of the page — shows the same scene fully colored in a cheerful, saturated palette, serving as a color reference guide. This mini version uses flat, bright colors with no gradients, making it easy for the colorist to identify which hues to apply to which areas. The overall layout is clean, with a thin decorative border framing the entire page.

## Variables

- `{scene}`: The subject of the coloring page — e.g., "a treehouse village connected by rope bridges, with animal characters having a tea party," "an underwater kingdom with a mermaid palace and sea creatures," "a space station orbiting a ringed planet with astronaut cats"
- `{target_audience}`: Age group and skill level — e.g., "children ages 4-6, with large simple shapes and minimal fine detail," "older children ages 8-12, with moderate detail and small pattern areas," "adults, with intricate patterns and dense compositions"
- `{complexity}`: Level of line detail — e.g., "low, with bold outlines and large fill areas," "medium, with some interior patterns and varied shape sizes," "high, with dense linework, fine details, and mandala-like pattern fills"
- `{theme}`: Overarching thematic concept — e.g., "enchanted woodland fantasy," "retro-futuristic space adventure," "cozy autumn harvest"

## Aspect Ratio
3:4

## Usage Notes
This template produces print-ready coloring pages with a built-in color guide, ideal for coloring books, classroom activities, or printable content. Nano Banana Pro (gemini-3.1-flash-image-preview) produces clean linework, though results may need minor cleanup for truly print-ready output. Specify the target audience clearly, as this dramatically affects line weight and detail density. The color reference inset is the key differentiator of this template.
