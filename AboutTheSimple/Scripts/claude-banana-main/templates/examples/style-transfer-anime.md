# Style Transfer - Anime

## Domain Mode
Anime Art

## Technique
Anime/Ghibli Conversion

## Prompt Template

An anime-style illustration depicting {scene_description}, reimagined through the lens of {anime_style} with its characteristic approach to line work, color, and atmosphere. The palette emphasizes {color_mood} tones, with colors applied in soft, slightly desaturated washes that give the image a hand-painted cel animation warmth. The sky and open spaces feature the signature layered cloud formations and luminous gradients typical of Japanese animation backgrounds. {atmospheric_detail} fills the scene with a sense of transient beauty and quiet emotion. Characters and objects have clean outlines with subtle line weight variation, and faces feature simplified but expressive features with large reflective eyes. Light behaves gently — no harsh shadows, but rather soft ambient illumination that makes every surface glow faintly from within. The composition feels like a still frame pulled from the emotional climax of a film, inviting the viewer to imagine the moments before and after.

## Variables

- `{scene_description}`: The real-world scene being converted — e.g., "a teenage girl waiting at a rural train station at sunset, holding a straw hat," "a fisherman mending nets on a weathered dock at dawn"
- `{anime_style}`: Specific anime aesthetic reference — e.g., "Studio Ghibli with Miyazaki's pastoral softness," "Makoto Shinkai's luminous sky-focused realism," "Satoshi Kon's urban psychological tone"
- `{color_mood}`: Emotional color direction — e.g., "warm amber and rose gold sunset," "cool twilight indigo with pops of lantern orange," "spring green and cherry blossom pink"
- `{atmospheric_detail}`: Environmental storytelling element — e.g., "cherry blossom petals drifting lazily on the wind," "distant rain curtains visible over the mountain range," "fireflies emerging from the tall grass at dusk"

## Aspect Ratio
16:9

## Usage Notes
This template transforms any scene description into a convincing anime aesthetic, particularly suited for Ghibli-inspired or Shinkai-inspired looks. Nano Banana Pro (gemini-3.1-flash-image-preview) and gemini-2.5-flash-image both handle anime style conversion with strong color and linework fidelity. For strongest results, include a human figure to anchor the emotional tone and choose atmospheric details that imply a narrative moment.

The palette emphasizes warm amber and rose gold sunset tones, with colors applied in soft, slightly desaturated washes that give the image a hand-painted cel animation warmth.
