# Chibi Transformation

## Domain Mode
Character Design

## Technique
Chibi/Q-Version Transformation

## Prompt Template

A cute 3D chibi figure of {person_description}, rendered in a glossy vinyl collectible style with an oversized head roughly twice the size of the body. The figure is wearing {outfit}, and the proportions are exaggerated with stubby little limbs, rounded fingers, and large sparkling eyes that convey warmth and playfulness. The chibi is posed in a {pose} stance, standing on a small circular display base with a subtle gradient. The setting around the figure is {setting}, rendered in soft pastel tones that complement the figure without overwhelming it. The surface of the figure has a smooth, slightly reflective finish reminiscent of high-end designer toys. Soft studio lighting wraps around the form, casting gentle shadows beneath the base. The overall mood is cheerful, collectible, and endearing, as though this figure belongs in a limited-edition capsule series.

## Variables

- `{person_description}`: Physical appearance and distinguishing features — e.g., "a young woman with long red braids and freckles," "a bearded blacksmith with broad shoulders"
- `{outfit}`: Clothing and accessories — e.g., "a yellow raincoat with tiny rubber boots," "full samurai armor with a miniature katana"
- `{pose}`: Body language and gesture — e.g., "peace sign with a wink," "sitting cross-legged reading a tiny book"
- `{setting}`: Surrounding environment or backdrop — e.g., "a miniature cherry blossom garden," "a simple white void with confetti particles"

## Aspect Ratio
1:1

## Usage Notes
This template converts any character description into a charming collectible-style chibi figure. Nano Banana Pro (gemini-3.1-flash-image-preview) achieves the vinyl toy aesthetic with strong material rendering and soft studio lighting. Best used when you want to create merchandise concepts, profile avatars, or gift illustrations.

## Example Output

A cute 3D chibi figure of a young woman with long red braids, freckles, and bright green eyes, rendered in a glossy vinyl collectible style with an oversized head roughly twice the size of the body. The figure is wearing a yellow raincoat with tiny rubber boots and holding a miniature umbrella, and the proportions are exaggerated with stubby little limbs, rounded fingers, and large sparkling eyes that convey warmth and playfulness. The chibi is posed in a peace sign with a wink stance, standing on a small circular display base with a subtle gradient. The setting around the figure is a miniature cherry blossom garden with petite pink trees and a tiny stone path, rendered in soft pastel tones that complement the figure without overwhelming it. The surface of the figure has a smooth, slightly reflective finish reminiscent of high-end designer toys. Soft studio lighting wraps around the form, casting gentle shadows beneath the base. The overall mood is cheerful, collectible, and endearing, as though this figure belongs in a limited-edition capsule series.
