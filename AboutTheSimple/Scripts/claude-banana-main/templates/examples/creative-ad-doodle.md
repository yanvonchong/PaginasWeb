# Creative Ad Doodle

## Domain Mode
Advertising Design

## Technique
Hand-Drawn Doodles + Real Object

## Prompt Template

A product photography shot of {product} placed on a clean, simple surface and photographed with crisp commercial lighting, surrounded by playful hand-drawn doodle illustrations that extend the product's story into an imaginative world. The doodles are rendered in {doodle_concept} style, drawn as if someone sketched directly onto the photograph with a thick marker or pen, adding whimsical illustrated characters, motion lines, speech bubbles, arrows, and decorative flourishes that interact with the real product in clever ways. The illustrated elements appear to emerge from, lean against, sit on top of, or react to the photographed product, creating a seamless blend of real and drawn. The text "{ad_text}" appears integrated into the doodle art as hand-lettered typography, looking as though it was scribbled naturally alongside the illustrations. The brand identity of {brand} is subtly present through consistent color choices and the overall tone of the doodles — fun and approachable but polished enough for commercial use. The contrast between the crisp product photography and the loose hand-drawn art creates an energetic, youthful, and shareable visual.

## Variables

- `{product}`: The real photographed item — e.g., "a matte black travel coffee mug with a bamboo lid," "a pair of white wireless earbuds in their charging case," "a glass jar of orange marmalade"
- `{doodle_concept}`: Theme and style of the illustrations — e.g., "adventure and exploration with tiny mountain climbers scaling the mug and a drawn helicopter above," "music and rhythm with dancing stick figures, floating music notes, and sound waves," "garden and nature with vines, bees, and sunshine radiating from the jar"
- `{ad_text}`: Marketing copy or tagline — e.g., "Fuel Your Journey," "Sound That Moves You," "Sunshine In A Jar"
- `{brand}`: Brand name and personality — e.g., "TrailBrew, an outdoor lifestyle brand with earthy green and brown tones," "BeatPods, a youth-oriented audio brand with electric purple accents," "Nana's Grove, a wholesome artisan food brand with warm yellow tones"

## Aspect Ratio
4:5

## Usage Notes
This template creates engaging product advertisements that feel organic, fun, and highly shareable on social media. Nano Banana Pro (gemini-3.1-flash-image-preview) handles the mixed-media look of real photography plus illustrated overlays convincingly. The doodle style should feel spontaneous — overly refined illustrations lose the charm. Keep the product itself clean and well-lit so it remains the hero of the composition.

## Example Output

A product photography shot of a matte black travel coffee mug with a bamboo lid placed on a light wooden table and photographed with crisp commercial lighting, surrounded by playful hand-drawn doodle illustrations that extend the mug's story into an imaginative adventure world. The doodles are rendered in an adventure and exploration style with tiny illustrated mountain climbers scaling the side of the mug using drawn ropes, a sketched helicopter hovering above the bamboo lid trailing a banner, little campfire drawings on the table surface, and a doodled mountain range stretching out behind the mug like a panoramic backdrop, drawn as if someone sketched directly onto the photograph with a thick black marker, adding whimsical characters, motion lines, and decorative pine trees that interact with the real mug in clever ways. The illustrated climbers appear to grip the mug's real edge, and a drawn trail of steam from the lid transforms into a sketched cloud with a tiny flag on top. The text "Fuel Your Journey" appears integrated into the doodle art as hand-lettered typography arcing above the helicopter banner, looking as though it was scribbled naturally alongside the illustrations. The brand identity of TrailBrew, an outdoor lifestyle brand, is subtly present through consistent earthy green and brown tones in the doodle ink and the overall adventurous but approachable tone. The contrast between the crisp mug photography and the loose hand-drawn mountain adventure creates an energetic, youthful, and shareable visual.
