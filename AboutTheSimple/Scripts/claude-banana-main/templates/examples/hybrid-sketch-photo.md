# Hybrid Sketch-Photo

## Domain Mode
Mixed Media Art

## Technique
Pencil Sketch + Lifelike Contrast

## Prompt Template

A mixed-media composition where {sketch_scene} is rendered entirely in loose, expressive pencil sketch lines on cream-toned paper, with visible graphite textures, cross-hatching for shadows, and rough construction lines left intentionally visible. Within this hand-drawn world, {realistic_element} is depicted in full full-color lifelike color, standing out dramatically against the monochrome sketched environment. The realistic element carries a vivid {color_accent} tone that draws the eye immediately, creating a striking focal contrast between the two visual languages. The composition follows a {composition} arrangement, guiding the viewer's gaze from the sketched periphery toward the full-color lifelike anchor. The pencil lines occasionally overlap onto the realistic element at the edges, blurring the boundary between drawn and real. The paper texture is visible throughout, with subtle coffee-stain rings and eraser smudges adding authenticity to the sketchbook feel.

## Variables

- `{sketch_scene}`: The hand-drawn environment and context — e.g., "a bustling Parisian street with cafe awnings and lampposts," "an underwater coral reef with fish and seaweed"
- `{realistic_element}`: The single full-color lifelike object or subject — e.g., "a red vintage bicycle leaning against a sketched wall," "a golden clownfish swimming among drawn anemones"
- `{color_accent}`: Dominant color of the realistic element — e.g., "cherry red," "deep ocean blue," "warm amber"
- `{composition}`: Layout and framing approach — e.g., "rule-of-thirds with the realistic element at left intersection," "centered with radial sketch lines converging inward"

## Aspect Ratio
3:2

## Usage Notes
This template creates a compelling visual contrast between hand-drawn and full-color lifelike elements, perfect for editorial illustrations, book covers, or conceptual art pieces. It performs best with Nano Banana Pro (gemini-3.1-flash-image-preview) at 2K resolution, where the model can differentiate between rendering styles within a single image. Choose a realistic element with strong color identity for maximum impact.

## Example Output

Within this hand-drawn world, a red vintage bicycle leaning against a sketched stone wall is depicted in full full-color lifelike color, standing out dramatically against the monochrome sketched environment. The realistic element carries a vivid cherry red tone that draws the eye immediately, creating a striking focal contrast between the two visual languages. The composition follows a rule-of-thirds arrangement with the bicycle at the left intersection, guiding the viewer's gaze from the sketched periphery toward the full-color lifelike anchor. The pencil lines occasionally overlap onto the bicycle's handlebars at the edges, blurring the boundary between drawn and real.
