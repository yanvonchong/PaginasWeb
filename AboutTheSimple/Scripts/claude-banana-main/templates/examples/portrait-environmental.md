# Portrait Environmental

## Domain Mode
Portrait

## Technique
Wide-angle environmental storytelling with available light

## Prompt Template

An environmental portrait of {subject} situated within {environment}, engaged in {activity}. The camera pulls back to a wider focal length — a 35mm lens — allowing the surrounding space to become a co-narrator, revealing as much about the person through context as through their face. The available light is the primary illumination source, shaped by the architecture and geography of the space itself: light filtering through windows, bouncing off walls, pooling on work surfaces. The mood is {mood}, communicated through the interplay of the subject's posture, the objects that surround them, and the quality of light that fills the room. The subject is positioned at a natural convergence point within the environment, where leading lines in the architecture draw the viewer's eye. Focus is set on the subject with moderate depth of field at f/4, keeping the immediate surroundings legible while the far background softens gently. The color palette emerges organically from the environment, unmanipulated and authentic.

## Variables

- `{subject}`: The person and a key identifying detail — e.g., "a ceramicist with clay-dusted apron and calloused hands," "a retired fisherman with sun-weathered skin and a cable-knit sweater," "a young architect surrounded by rolled blueprints"
- `{environment}`: The meaningful space — e.g., "their cluttered pottery studio with a kick wheel and shelves of drying bowls," "a cramped fishing boat cabin with nets and tide charts pinned to the wall," "a modernist glass-walled office overlooking a construction site"
- `{activity}`: What they are doing — e.g., "trimming the foot of a freshly thrown bowl," "mending a net with practiced hands," "sketching structural details in a Moleskine notebook"
- `{mood}`: The emotional atmosphere — e.g., "quiet absorption," "weathered contentment," "focused intensity," "nostalgic stillness"

## Aspect Ratio
3:2

## Usage Notes
Environmental portraits tell a story that headshots cannot. This template works when the subject's identity is inseparable from their surroundings — artisans, professionals, people defined by place. The 35mm focal length is key: wide enough to include context, not so wide that the subject becomes incidental. Nano Banana Pro (gemini-3.1-flash-image-preview) handles environmental detail and natural light particularly well.
