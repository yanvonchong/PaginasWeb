# Logo Minimal

## Domain Mode
Logo

## Technique
Golden ratio geometric construction with negative space

## Prompt Template

A minimal brand mark for {brand_name}, a company in the {industry} sector. The logo is constructed using golden ratio proportions, with overlapping circles and arcs serving as the invisible scaffolding that defines every curve, angle, and junction in the design. The icon concept is rooted in {icon_concept}, abstracted to its most essential geometric form — recognizable in intent but not literal in execution. The color palette uses {color_palette}, applied as flat fills with no gradients, shadows, or dimensional effects. The mark must function at scales ranging from a favicon to a billboard, so every line and shape maintains clarity at both extremes. Negative space is used as an active design element, creating secondary readings or hidden forms within the mark that reward closer inspection. The logo sits on a clean white background, presented alongside a simple wordmark in a complementary geometric typeface set in medium weight. Construction lines and golden ratio circles are faintly visible as an overlay, revealing the mathematical precision underlying the organic-looking result.

## Variables

- `{brand_name}`: The company or product name — e.g., "Terraverde," "Nimbus Audio," "Koan Studio," "Helix Robotics"
- `{industry}`: The business domain — e.g., "sustainable agriculture," "consumer audio technology," "meditation and wellness," "industrial automation"
- `{icon_concept}`: The visual metaphor — e.g., "a leaf merging with a circuit board," "sound waves forming a mountain peak," "a spiral that implies both stillness and motion," "interlocking gears forming a double helix"
- `{color_palette}`: Flat color selections — e.g., "forest green and warm charcoal," "electric blue on white," "matte black monochrome," "terracotta and cream"

## Aspect Ratio
1:1

## Usage Notes
Logo generation through AI is best used for ideation and mood boarding, not final production assets. This template produces clean, geometrically grounded marks that communicate the golden-ratio construction approach clearly. Nano Banana Pro (gemini-3.1-flash-image-preview) handles logo prompts well when the style is explicitly described as flat and minimal. Avoid requesting text rendering in the logo — letterform accuracy remains a challenge for image models.
