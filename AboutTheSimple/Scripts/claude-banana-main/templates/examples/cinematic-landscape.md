# Cinematic Landscape

## Domain Mode
Cinema

## Technique
Anamorphic lens simulation with filmic color grading

## Prompt Template

A sweeping cinematic landscape of {location} during {time_of_day}, captured with an anamorphic 40mm lens on 35mm film stock. The {weather} conditions shape the atmosphere, casting volumetric light through the environment and creating natural lens flares that streak horizontally across the frame. The composition follows a deep staging approach with a clearly defined foreground, midground, and background, each layer receding into atmospheric perspective. The overall mood is {mood}, reinforced by a color grade that shifts between cool shadows and warm highlights. Practical light sources within the scene — whether distant fires, city glow, or celestial bodies — provide motivated illumination that grounds the image in cinematic realism. The aspect ratio is ultra-wide, evoking the scope of a major studio production shot on location. Fine grain texture is visible throughout, lending an organic, celluloid quality to every surface.

## Variables

- `{location}`: The primary environment or geography — e.g., "the Scottish Highlands," "a flooded Venetian piazza," "the Namibian desert"
- `{time_of_day}`: Lighting period — e.g., "golden hour," "blue hour," "deep midnight," "overcast noon"
- `{weather}`: Atmospheric condition — e.g., "rolling fog," "a clearing thunderstorm," "light snowfall," "dry heat haze"
- `{mood}`: Emotional tone — e.g., "melancholic solitude," "quiet grandeur," "ominous tension," "serene wonder"

## Aspect Ratio
21:9

## Usage Notes
Best suited for establishing shots, wallpaper-style compositions, or any project requiring cinematic scale. Nano Banana Pro (gemini-3.1-flash-image-preview) renders anamorphic lens language and film grain with strong fidelity at 2K resolution. Pair with a low camera angle for maximum dramatic impact.
