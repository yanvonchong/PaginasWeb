# Editorial Fashion

## Domain Mode
Editorial

## Technique
Full-body fashion narrative with directional color story

## Prompt Template

A full-body editorial fashion photograph of {model_description} wearing {outfit_details}, shot on location at {location} during {lighting_time}. The styling is deliberate and story-driven, every garment and accessory chosen to build a character rather than merely display clothing. The model's pose is dynamic yet controlled — weight shifted to one hip, chin slightly elevated, hands engaged with purpose rather than dangling passively. The directional light of the hour rakes across the scene at a low angle, creating long shadows and rich tonal contrast that sculpts the fabric and reveals its drape and construction. The background environment is integral to the narrative, not incidental — architectural lines, natural textures, and environmental color all echo and amplify the wardrobe's story. Shot on a medium format digital back with a 110mm lens, the image balances fashion-forward composition with fine-art sensibility. The color story is tightly controlled: no more than three dominant hues plus neutrals, creating a palette that feels curated without appearing artificial. Post-processing is restrained, with accurate skin tones and fabric colors preserved.

## Variables

- `{model_description}`: The model's appearance — e.g., "a tall woman with dark skin and close-cropped natural hair," "a lean androgynous figure with angular cheekbones and platinum buzz cut," "a broad-shouldered man with a thick beard and deep brown eyes"
- `{outfit_details}`: Complete wardrobe description — e.g., "an oversized camel wool coat over a white cotton turtleneck, wide-leg charcoal trousers, and chunky-soled black leather boots," "a floor-length emerald silk slip dress with a deconstructed denim jacket draped over one shoulder," "a structured linen jumpsuit in sand, cinched with a woven leather belt, paired with open-toe mules"
- `{location}`: The shoot location — e.g., "a brutalist concrete parking structure," "a sun-bleached Greek island staircase with whitewashed walls," "an abandoned Art Deco cinema lobby with faded gilt molding"
- `{lighting_time}`: The light quality and time — e.g., "the last fifteen minutes of golden hour," "flat overcast midday light," "harsh noon sun creating graphic shadows," "early morning blue light before sunrise"

## Aspect Ratio
2:3

## Usage Notes
This template targets the specific visual language of high-end fashion editorials — the kind published in print magazines with a clear point of view. It works best when the outfit details are specific and layered, not generic. Nano Banana Pro (gemini-3.1-flash-image-preview) handles fashion compositions with particular sophistication. For best results, ensure the location and outfit share a coherent visual story rather than clashing arbitrarily.
