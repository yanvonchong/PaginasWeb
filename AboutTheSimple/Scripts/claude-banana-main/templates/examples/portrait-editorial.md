# Portrait Editorial

## Domain Mode
Portrait

## Technique
Rembrandt lighting with shallow depth of field

## Prompt Template

An editorial portrait of {subject} wearing {outfit}, photographed in {setting} with the refined sensibility of a {publication_reference} cover story. The lighting follows a Rembrandt pattern — a single modified key light positioned high and forty-five degrees to one side, producing the signature triangle of light on the shadowed cheek. A subtle hair light adds separation from the background, while ambient environmental light provides context without competing with the portrait lighting. The subject's expression is unguarded, caught between poses, conveying personality rather than performance. The lens is an 85mm f/1.4 shot wide open, rendering the background into a painterly wash of color and shape that suggests the environment without defining it. Skin tones are rendered with warmth and accuracy, preserving natural texture. The composition places the subject off-center using the rule of thirds, with gaze direction creating visual tension against the frame edge. Color grading leans toward muted, desaturated tones with lifted blacks.

## Variables

- `{subject}`: The person being photographed — e.g., "a woman in her sixties with silver cropped hair and laugh lines," "a young man with freckles and deep-set green eyes," "a nonbinary dancer with close-shaved head and angular features"
- `{outfit}`: Wardrobe details — e.g., "a draped ivory silk blouse with an oversized collar," "a tailored navy double-breasted suit, no tie, top button open," "a vintage hand-knit cardigan over a plain white tee"
- `{setting}`: The location — e.g., "a sun-washed Parisian apartment with floor-to-ceiling windows," "a wood-paneled library with green banker's lamps," "a rooftop greenhouse at dusk"
- `{publication_reference}`: The editorial tone — e.g., "Kinfolk magazine," "The Gentlewoman," "Aperture quarterly," "Vogue Italia's Talents section"

## Aspect Ratio
3:4

## Usage Notes
This template produces portraits with editorial gravitas — the kind used for magazine profiles, author photos, or brand ambassador campaigns. The Rembrandt lighting reference produces reliable, sophisticated results with Nano Banana Pro (gemini-3.1-flash-image-preview) and gemini-2.5-flash-image. Avoid combining with action or full-body framing; this is a head-and-shoulders composition.
